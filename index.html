The Guide

Meet Priya Chaudhary

🌸
B1 · Rishikesh
L2 · Yin Certified
"THE BODY KNOWS WHAT THE MIND FORGETS"

Priya Chaudhary found yoga during a period of profound stillness — and never looked back. Trained in Rishikesh under classical Hatha lineages, she holds a 500hr RYT certification and additional training in Yin, Restorative, and Pranayama.

Her classes are raw, direct, and deeply humane. She believes yoga is not performance — it is investigation. Each session is an invitation to arrive, exactly as you are.
